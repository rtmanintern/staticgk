          (i) STOP (ii) DRIOA
          (iii) PENOH (iv) DREAGEN

          • A
          • B
          • C
          • D

          आपका जवाब गलत है

          Explanation:
          STOP = POST
          DRIOA = RADIO
          PENOH = PHONE
          DREAGEN = GRENADE

                  • A
                  • B
                  • C
                  • D

                  Explanation:
